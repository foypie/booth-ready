FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for artists purchasing, releasing, and crediting Booth Ready instrumentals.

Who produces the beats?

Booth Ready instrumentals are produced by Beatz4Life, LLC and crafted by Mista Foy.

What credit should I use?

Use: Prod. by Mista Foy for Beatz4Life, LLC.

Can I resell the beat or claim ownership?

No. You may use the beat according to your purchased license, but you may not resell, redistribute, or claim ownership of the instrumental unless ownership rights are expressly transferred in a written Exclusive License Agreement.

How do I ask about exclusive rights?

Use the contact section to request exclusive rights. Final terms should be confirmed in writing before release.
